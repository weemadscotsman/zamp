### SCAM 1: LUMINARY OASIS
**Type:** Essential Oil Wellness MLM
**Target:** Health-conscious, isolated parents
**Hook:** "Be your own boss, heal your family"

**Products:**
- "Quantum Balance" essential oils ($89/bottle)
- "Detoxifying" crystals ($150)
- "Alignment" journals ($45)
- Starter kit: $299

**Recruitment Script:**
```
"I used to be tired, sick, and broke like you.
Then I discovered the power of plant medicine.
Now I work from my phone while my kids nap!
My upline bought a Tesla last month.
The oils basically sell themselves.
You just have to believe in abundance!"
```

**Red Flags:**
- Claims oils cure diseases
- "Do your own research" (against FDA)
- Must buy monthly to stay active
- 99% of sellers make <$100/year

**Collapse Event:** FDA warning letter, mass refund requests

### SCAM 2: BIT-KONNEXX
**Type:** Crypto Lending Ponzi
**Target:** Young men, tech-curious, FOMO-driven
**Hook:** "Guaranteed 1% daily returns"

**The Pitch:**
```
"Revolutionary trading bot!
AI-powered algorithm!
Just deposit $1000 and watch it grow!
Compound interest is the 8th wonder!
Refer friends and earn MORE!
HEY HEY HEYYYY!"
```

**Mechanics:**
- Daily "returns" shown in dashboard
- Lock-up periods prevent withdrawal
- Referral bonuses (pyramid structure)
- Fake trading history

**Red Flags:**
- Guaranteed returns (impossible)
- No explanation of how it works
- Aggressive marketing events
- Anonymous "founders"

**Collapse Event:** Platform "hacked," funds "frozen," exit scam

### SCAM 4: PASSIVE PROFESSOR
**Type:** Dropshipping Course Pyramid
**Target:** Aspiring entrepreneurs, desperate for escape
**Hook:** "Learn the secret to $10k/month passive income"

**The Pitch:**
```
"I was a broke barista.
Now I make money in my sleep!
No inventory, no shipping, no hassle!
Just copy my exact system!
Course is $997 (normally $4997)!
But only for ACTION TAKERS!"
```

**The Reality:**
- Course is generic YouTube advice
- "Secret supplier" is AliExpress
- Market saturated years ago
- Real money is selling courses TO YOU

**Red Flags:**
- Income from courses, not dropshipping
- Fake lifestyle (rented Airbnb)
- No refunds
- Upsells constantly

**Collapse Event:** Credit card chargebacks, FTC investigation
